App Features

Start working with Healthy Drinks App that can provide everything you need to get healthy, physical life.

Healthy Life

option that allows you to search a smoothie by indicating the ingredients you own and the app will tell you all the smoothies you can make in a second!

Improving Health

All recipes are illustrated and for each one you can find the ingredients list, nutritional value and instructions for preparation.

Physical Activity

Detailed, easy-to-follow recipe instructions, and on the fly search recipes by ingredients or dish name (results change as you type).

Fitness & Performance

A recipe box to save your favorite recipes all in one place and add ingredients from recipe to the shopping list.

Balance Body

Add your own entries in the shopping list (one tap mark as purchased, long tap delete entry) .

Balance Mind

Submit your own recipes to be published in the app (will be checked and published if appropriate) .

Eating Well

Search by Type of diet, Taste buds, course, eating time and much more and Bookmark your favourite recipes.

Workout Routines

Food, Beauty, Wellness and Household Remedies and Filter To ignore what you don’t want to come in search.

Your Own Privacy

User community and Private Area for your own notes, preferences .
